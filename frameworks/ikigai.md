Ikigai

Use when: Career decisions, life-direction questions.

How: Find the intersection of what you love, what you're good at, what the world needs, what you can be paid for. Diagnose which dimension is missing when something feels off.

Apply: Ask "Which of these four feels weakest right now?"
- Has passion but no income → missing monetization
- Has skills and income but feels empty → missing love
- Loves the work but feels pointless → missing world need
- Wants to help but can't sustain → missing skills or income

Source: Japanese concept, Okinawan longevity research. García & Miralles (2016).

Cross-validates with: Regret Minimization (Ikigai finds direction, Regret Minimization tests conviction).
