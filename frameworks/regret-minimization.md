Regret Minimization

Use when: High-stakes irreversible decisions where risk aversion battles aspiration.

How: Project to age 80. Ask "Will I regret not having tried this?" Bypasses short-term fear by anchoring on long-term identity. Humans regret inaction more than action.

Apply:
1. Name the decision
2. Imagine yourself at 80 looking back
3. Which choice would you regret more — trying and failing, or never trying?

Source: Bezos. Grounded in Gilovich & Medvec, Cornell (1995).

Cross-validates with: Ikigai (finds direction), Pre-Mortem (stress-tests the path after conviction is confirmed).
